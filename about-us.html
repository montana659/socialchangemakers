Our Story

We promote equity & inclusion across Canada. We are building a community of youth that support each other, network, grow and learn together as we all word to address thee social problems that exist in our world, and create a more accepting, inclusive society.

Our Team

Social Changemakers are youth leaders from across Canada who are working towards creating positive social change, nationally and globally. Through the Harmony Movement-Social Changemakers Program youth are trained to critically analyze the world they live and given the tools to learn how to identify current issues, create inativites, and take action.

Our Philisophy

As Social Changemakers we promote equity & inclusion across Canada. We use the 3Rs of Social Change to address social problems that exist in our world.

“Equality is treating everyone the same, while equity is recognizing that everyone is different and giving everyone what they need to achieve the same result.”
