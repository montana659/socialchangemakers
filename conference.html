Social Changemakers Conference Series

What is the Social Changemakers Leadership Series?

Delivered completely online, this immersive conference series will bring together young leaders from across Canada from a range of backgrounds, faiths, abilities, genders, sexual orientations, including Indigenous and Francophone youth. The leaders will network, build understanding, and create connections with each other. They will examine the current state of diversity and inclusion and identify issues they want to address in their local communities. The program will include speakers, workshops, virtual field trips, and individual and group reflections!

What Will The Social Changemakers Conference Series Look Like?

The Social Changemakers Conference Series will be delivered completely online to avoid any financial, geographic, medical, or accessibility barriers that participants may face. The Emerging Leaders (ages 18-24) will design the contents of the conference, but the general topics include how to progress diversity, equity, and inclusion in communities. The Youth Conference portion will bring together student teams (aged 14-18) to learn how to identify issues and take actions to create positive change in their schools and communities.

During this conference we'll discus:

  • The difference between social change and charity
  • Using your story to create social change
  • Challenging our own bias
  • Learning how to connect with people with different perspectives

    Who Should Apply?

    There are three streams to the Social Changemakers Leadership Series.

    Student Teams

    Student Teams are composed of up to ten youth (ages 14-19) from each province and territory, with an optional adult monitor/supervisor for each group. Student Teams will attend the SCC to expand their understanding of Canada’s history, their own identity, and take action to improve their communities. The conference will take place virtually from March 4-5, 2022, facilitated by the Emerging Leaders team under the mentorship and guidance of the Harmony Movement Staff.

    The deadline to apply is February 11, 2022. Once accepted, student teams will be required to pay a $25 participation fee. The fee can be waived if it is a barrier to participation.

    Apply As A Student Team

    Individual Youth

    Join us and get connected with other youth in high school (ages 14-19) from each province and territory. There is no cost to register to attend.

    The deadline to apply is February 11, 2022.

    Apply As An Individual Youth

    Emerging Leaders

    The SCC will bring together 26 Emerging Leaders (ages 18-24), two from each territory and province, who have demonstrated commitment to advancing equity and inclusion within their communities. These leaders will collaborate to plan and facilitate a virtual conference series for high school youth. Emerging Leaders will meet biweekly to prepare for the SCC from October 2021 to January 2022. Emerging Leaders will facilitate their conference series to Student Teams and Youth over a multiple-week period in January 2022, under the mentorship and guidance of the Harmony Movement staff. A small honorarium of $150 will be paid to each Emerging Leader for their participation.

    Apply As Emerging Leader
